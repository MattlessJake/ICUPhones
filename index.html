Date: 20/02/19

Samsung has recently announced it's delve in to the world of foldable phones, and what they came up with has been dubbed the 'Samsung Galaxy Fold'.

This phone is said to have four colours to choose from: Space Silver, Cosmos Black, Martian Green and Astro Blue. The screen of the phone when folded has been stated to be 4.6 Inces, while when the phone is un-folded is said to have a 7.3 Inch Display. The price is estimated to be around $2000 on release.
